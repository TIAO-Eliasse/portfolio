Who I Am

Eliasse is an M.Sc. AI student at AIMS, Google DeepMind Scholar, and trained Statistician-Economist. His research focuses on Explainable AI (XAI), NLP, and probabilistic forecasting for decision-making systems.


He is co-author of a Camera-Ready paper at SemEval-2026 (ACL Anthology) on polarization detection using transformer-based LLMs. His journey from Burkina Faso to Cape Town reflects a commitment to resilience and AI excellence.
